Why Is There No Contact Form?

Cheapcondoinsurance.com does not use a contact form because we feel a phone call is ultimately more convenient for you. Other insurance websites request that you fill in a long contact form. They then give your phone number to 10 or more call centers who will keep calling and annoying you. We don't want to hound you with phone calls. If you want an insurance quote, call us 9-6PST and we'll give you one on the spot with no strings attached. As it should be.

What Does Hassle-Free Mean?

It means you call up and get your quote. It's as simple as that. We don't do high-pressure sales and we won't keep you on the phone for hours.

Where Is Your Call Center?

Our call center is located in Lancaster, California. We are insurance experts who believe our best customers are well informed and satisfied with our service.
